About Composer Atlas

A strategy reference for Composer.trade investors.

Composer Atlas is a reference library for systematic investors using Composer.trade — a platform for building and running rules-based investment symphonies.

This site presents backtested performance data, strategy logic breakdowns, and educational explanations of the concepts behind systematic investing — making it easier to evaluate and understand algorithmic strategies before putting real capital to work.

What's Here

  • 24 Composer.trade strategy symphonies with full backtested metrics
  • Strategy logic breakdowns: signals, conditions, and exit rules
  • Glossary of indicators, risk metrics, and strategy concepts
  • Cross-linked concepts — every strategy links to relevant glossary terms

Data & Disclaimers

All performance metrics are backtested — derived from historical simulation, not live trading results. Backtested performance does not guarantee or predict future returns.

Nothing on this site constitutes financial advice, investment advice, or a recommendation to buy or sell any security or strategy. Use this information as a starting point for your own research.
